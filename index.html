About Me

Get to know the person behind the code

I'm Dwayne Mhlanga, known online as dwaX or dwaXGamer - a passionate young creator with a deep love for technology, design, cybersecurity, and God. I was raised in a strong Christian foundation, and that shaped who I am: creative, disciplined, and driven by purpose.

I completed my National Diploma in Information Technology, while also studying and building a solid foundation in Cybersecurity. Over the years, I've grown from a curious kid who loved gadgets and gaming into a developer who builds real digital solutions.

I design clean, functional websites, create brand identities, and build web-based systems. My work blends creativity and logical thinking - I enjoy taking an idea and turning it into something visually beautiful and technically reliable.

Beyond tech, I'm a writer at heart. I'm working on multiple books including "Seasons and Growth" and "A Letter to My Future Wife," where I combine faith, storytelling, and life lessons. I also run a blog, Geeked for Christ, where I share devotionals, tech content, and personal reflections.

At my core, I'm a believer, a problem solver, a builder, and someone who wants to inspire others through the gifts God has placed in me. I design with intention, I create with excellence, and I aim to grow in every season of my life.

Tech, faith, creativity, and purpose - that's who I am.
